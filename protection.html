Surge Protection Device

Surge protection – Single-family house with external lightning protection

Surge protective devices protect electric / electronic systems and devices from damage caused by excessively high voltages. This dangerous interference energy is gradually broken down to a level corresponding to the rated impulse voltage.

3 Phase and Single Phase SPD

Dehn Part No. 900360 (DVA CSP 3P 100 FM)
Dehn Part No. 900351 (DVA M NG IP 50 FM)
  • Prewired type 1 + type 2 spark-gap-based combined arrester, optimised for protecting mobile radio systems
  • Maximum system availability thanks to Rapid Arc Control / RADAX Flow follow current limitation
  • Discharge capacity up to 100 kA (10/350 µs)
  • No tripping of 32 A gG fuses up to short-circuit currents of 25 kArms
  • Protection of terminal devices within 10m
  • Visual status indication integrated in the module (green = ok, red = faulty); status is signalled via remote signalling contact
  • Different products tailored to the specific requirements of mobile radio
