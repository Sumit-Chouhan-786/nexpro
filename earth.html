Earth Electrode Solutions

Earthing – Single-family house with external lightning protection. Whether existing or new buildings, a functioning earthing system is the prerequisite for the safe operation of electric systems – buildings and for protecting people from dangerous high voltages. The design and implementation of the earth-termination system are of central importance. When implementing the earthing concept, e.g. in the form of a foundation earth electrode, changes are no longer possible after the concrete has been set.

Omissions or errors during the construction phase cannot be corrected later, or at least not without great effort and expense. Carrying out an earthing study is a mandatory requirement for every new installation project. The purpose of earthing in the electrical network is primarily to ensure safety. When all metallic parts of electrical equipment are grounded, there will be no effect due to dangerous voltages present within the equipment case.

As earthing enables the discharge of the excess charges through the ground. Although if there is a failure or situation arising inside the equipment, earthing prevents the buildup of electrical charges on the equipment body discharging to the ground. Nexpo has built its in-house capabilities in performing Earthing Study based on the latest standards IS 3043, IEEE 80, and more.

Green backfill earthing compound.

WORLD’S FIRST FULGURITES FREE BACKFILL SOLUTION

  • Fulgurites free backfill material
  • Tested for lightning current
  • Complies with IEC 62561-7 and IEEE Std 80
  • Non leachable solution for flood prone areas

  • Tested by TÜV Rheinland

DEHNterra is a fulgurites free, and green backfill earthing solution. The solution is critically synthesized to be compactable with a very good flow percentage value to ease out the installation of the earth pit. DEHNterra has been designed to increase the contact area between earth rod and inner wall of the earth pit.

High grade earthing solution

DEHN Terra is a versatile solution in order to provide the least resistance path for the short circuit and fault currents to the ground. It is a maintenance free green earthing solution which is soil compacting to ease out the installation of the earth pits. This is a naturally inert solution for protecting the environment and increasing the lifetime of earth pit by having low and stable resistivity in the range of 0.02 – 0.12 Ω·cm, as required by the standards.

Low Ground Resistance

An important requirement for a perfect earth enhancement material is to maintain a low grounding resistance, with minimal fluctuation for a long period of time. DEHN Terra is a robust solution which is checked and found helpful in reducing and maintaining the earth resistance.
